Frequently Asked Questions

What cleaning services do you offer?

We offer Standard Cleaning, Deep Cleaning, Move In/Out, Commercial & Office, Construction & Real Estate, Church & Ministry, and Professional Organization services. Each service includes a detailed checklist tailored to your needs.

How is pricing determined?

Prices are individually quoted based on the square footage and condition of your property. Use our free estimator for a ballpark range, then we'll provide an exact quote after a walk-through.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Venmo, Cash App, and cash. Payment is required before or at time of service. We do not accept credit cards at this time.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 72 hours' notice for cancellations. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a $25 fee. Same-day cancellations are charged 50% of the quoted price after the one-time grace period.

Do you use non-toxic products?

Yes! We are committed to using non-toxic cleaning products to ensure the safety and well-being of everyone in your home or business, including children and pets.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the Hall, Jackson, and Gwinnett County areas in Georgia. Contact us to confirm service availability in your area.

Do I need to be home during cleaning?

No, but we do need access to the property. If the cleaner is unable to enter due to a locked door, wrong code, or unnotified gate attendant, the full quote will be charged.
